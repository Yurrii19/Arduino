Title:
HydroSense: Arduino-Based Automated Sink Declogging System

Components:
Arduino Uno
Servo Motor
Stepper Motor
Water Level Motor

Steps:
1. A water level sensor is installed on the side of the sink. When the water stays above the set point for 3 minutes, the system automatically activates.

2. A container at the top holds colored water for demonstration purposes (replacing liquid sosa). A 180° servo motor opens to release the liquid onto the clog area.

3. A stepper motor at the sink drain activates to open the pathway, allowing the liquid to reach and clear the clog.

4. After the process completes, all motors stop.
