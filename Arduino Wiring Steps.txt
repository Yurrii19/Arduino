Arduino to Breadboard
5V to Positive Rail
GND to Negative Rail

LED Display
GND - Breadboard Negative
VCC - Breadboard Positive
SDA - Analog Pin A4
SCL - Analog Pin A5

Water Level Sensor
S - Analog Pin A0
+ - Breadboard Positive
- - Breadboard Negative

Servo Motor
Red - Breadboard Positive
Orange - Digital Pin 4
Brown - Breadboard Negative

Stepper Motor via Driver Module (must be powered in a external power supply)
